Internal monologue
In a play, a character speaks out his or her inner activities or feelings. These lines are spoken only to the audience and are not heard by other characters.
Language monologue
In a play,a character speaks his or her mind directly to the audience and other characters.
narratage
In drama, supplementary notes beyond the scenes for the purpose of rendering humorous or dramatic effects are only for the audience, and the contents are generally the feelings, thoughts and some background information of the characters.
